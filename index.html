Welcome to BusMall | Voting

At BusMall we strive to bring you the content you are passionate about. Our articles always try to reflect your interests and to provide you with the information you need. This is where BusMall | Voting comes in. Every month we will let you decide what or who next month's cover article will be about!


We all love to play in one way or another. It's human nature. A lot of us satisfy this need with video games. The gaming industry has reached an all-time high and popular game characters have turned into superstars. Therefor next month's issue of BusMall will feature a story about one of these characters. It is your decicion which one it will be!


Below we introduce 8 of the most famous and popular game characters in current gaming. These eight will battle for a spot in our magazine on the Voting Page. Once you know who is in the running please go to our Voting page (you can find the link on the top of this page). There you get to choose from 3 images by clicking on your favorite. Once a round of voting is done you can either view the results or vote some more. We thank you for your participation and may the most popular character win!
